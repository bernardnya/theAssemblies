MINISTERIAL TRAINING AND MINISTERS PLACEMENT

Church structure is typically viewed as a hierarchy in the form of top overseers, then bishops, ministers and deacons, religious and laity structured on down through to the base of the line, other facets can be acknowledged. To be placed as bishop, one must have studied and acquired adequate biblical information and possessed other scientific knowledge to the doctoral or postdoctoral level.

Besides the identity of scriptural qualities of bishop, the competences of attitude, skill and knowledge enough to empower with the capacities to deliver essential public duties are required. Ministers are allowed to study relevant biblical information in recognized bible institutions theological seminaries, as well as universities that provide access to scriptural information essential to engaging sustainable ministry.

Deacons and others shall be involved in active ministry based on their quality of knowledge of social and spiritual development. All ministers and from bishops to others at the downline of ministry shall have basic education and training with the global education and training partnership program of the Assemblies.
