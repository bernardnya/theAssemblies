About Us

OUR VISION

To learn and live by the Holy Spirit with Christ and for God.

OUR AIM

To harness and reinforce the response to the great commission (Matthew 28:18-28, Mark 16:15-18); in extensive evangelism, soul winning, church planting, education and training of disciples in spiritual development, skills and talents engagement with social entrepreneurship development for human dignity, and ministry revivals.

MINISTER AND MINISTRIES

The hierarchy of the Assemblies consists of its bishops, ministers, and deacons. In the ecclesiological sense of the term, "hierarchy" strictly means the "holy ordering" of the church, the Body of Christ, so to respect the diversity of gifts and ministries necessary for genuine unity.
