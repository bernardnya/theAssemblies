FAITH FORCE (F2)

Family and Friends Fellowship are held at Homes with the Head of Service leading on a network system of spiritual service guided by the leaders of the Assemblies. Family and Friends Fellowship identifies with a working tool for Evangelistic Outreach Group shall be identified as “FAITH FORCE” Faith-force or F2 is a ministry service militia that embarks on active community outreach for preaching and teaching of the message of Christ Jesus as well as wins souls, plants churches and develop the community and church members both socially and spiritually.
