GLOBAL OUTREACH PROGRAMS

Global Ministry and Missions Education and Training

Embarkation on global mission initiatives and discipleship leads to the ascertaining of the Great Commission (evangelism, education, and training) of disciples into members and Ministers. Church planting and community development initiatives are also implemented, culminating in both social and spiritual development of the entire world. Project initiatives such as building of schools, hospitals, and universities are also adopted in the serving of the livelihood of humanity as a whole as well as the upholding of the concept of human dignity to the betterment of the world as a whole.

GUIDANCE & COUNSELLING

The Assemblies embarkation on counseling is subject to both spiritual and physical exposition into real-life issues of jurisdiction and jurisprudence where biblical concepts and ideologies are used in shaping the lives of Christians as well as the extension of these principles to the unsaved world. Thus, our meticulous counseling protocols are targeted at the winning of souls to the body of Christ.

Ministerial Ordination

Ministers of the Assemblies are subject to progressive scrutiny and vetting. The scheme for spiritual emphasis via activities of fasting and prayers is engaged relevant to the module of training required for continuing ministerial development and spiritual formation. This spiritual exercise sharpens their divine calling and endowment allowing them with their maximum of effort in ministerial delivery. Furthermore, ministers of the Assemblies must be professionals who otherwise have both the ability and capacity to care for their congregations with highly driven motivation of sacrifice and self-support.
